About Me

I'm a passionate advocate for equal access to justice. With a background in criminology, I'm especially interested in how digital platforms can simplify legal processes for the average Kenyan. I believe that understanding your rights shouldn’t be a privilege—it should be a basic service.

What I’m Working On

LegalConnect_KE

I’m currently building LegalConnect_KE—a platform to connect people with affordable legal help, including pro bono services. It’s a small step toward justice that’s truly accessible to all, especially the underserved communities in Kenya.

Why It Matters

More than 60% of low-income Kenyans face unresolved legal problems. By making legal advice easier to access, we help restore dignity and security to families, women, workers, and youth alike.
